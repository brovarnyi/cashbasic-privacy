Privacy Policy

cashBASIC — Personal Finance Tracker

Overview

cashBASIC is designed with your privacy as a priority. The app works entirely offline and does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data to external servers.

Data Storage

All your financial data — accounts, transactions, categories, and budgets — is stored locally on your device using Apple's SwiftData framework. Your data never leaves your device.

Data We Do Not Collect

  • No personal information (name, email, phone number)
  • No usage analytics or tracking
  • No location data
  • No device identifiers
  • No cookies or similar technologies

In-App Purchases

cashBASIC offers an optional tip jar powered by Apple's StoreKit. These transactions are processed entirely by Apple. We do not receive or store any payment information. Please refer to Apple's Privacy Policy for details on how Apple handles purchase data.

Third-Party Services

cashBASIC does not integrate any third-party SDKs, analytics services, advertising networks, or external APIs.

Children's Privacy

The app does not knowingly collect any data from anyone, including children under the age of 13.

Changes to This Policy

If this policy is updated, the revised version will be posted on this page with an updated effective date.
